dynARTwork GITHUB

GOAL OF THE PROJECT

The project was developed during the final examination of the Iot 2020 course. The goal of the project is to address a real-world problem within the context of Smart Museums. We want to distinguish ourselves from the two classic proposals: conservation of works of art, improvement of the visitor's user experience. We would like to create our work of art, or rather our generator of works of art. We think this is a way to attract visitors to the museum on a daily basis, as the artwork is dynamic and changes according to how the world changes.

THE IDEA

Our users are mainly artists so we will provide them this website in which they can register and they can load their artwork and choose the parameters In which they are interested in. The parameters are type of data which will be used in the creation process some examples could be pollution data, people feelings or acoustic noise. Thanks to machine learning our infrastructure will use the data collected by the IOT devices mixing them with the leaded image moreover the artists can see the result displayed on his screen finally the artwork will be projected in the museum room where it will contain to change dynamically
